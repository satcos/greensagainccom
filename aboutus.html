About Us

Green Saga Inc helps to decrease the impact on our environment by providing products manufactured with ecological resource (made from fallen areca leaves) through environmentally friendly process which is biodegradable and compostable.Our products are manufactured from fallen dry sheaths of Betel nut trees. The areca sheath when dried will fall from the tree and it is collected from the farms fresh and used for making products. Visit our online store to see a collection of Eco friendly areca leaf products and we are glad to customize products for bulk order.
